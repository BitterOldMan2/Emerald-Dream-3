About us

Emerald Dream Luxury is a holiday home in the heart of Faliraki, just 200 yards from the beach and 400 yards from the centre.The property boasts views of the garden and is 2.5 miles from Rhodes Waterpark.

Free WiFi is offered. This air-conditioned apartment features a kitchen with a dishwasher, an oven and a toaster, as well as a coffee machine. Towels and bed linen are featured in this holiday home. Other facilities at Emerald Dream Luxury include a sun terrace and a hot tub. A satellite TV is available with channels all over the world.
